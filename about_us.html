Know more about us...

The MEMM is a global environmental nonprofit working to create

a world where people and nature can thrive.

Founded in the U.S. through grassroots action in 1951, My Earth My Mother(MEMM) has grown to become one of the most effective and wide-reaching environmental organizations in the world. Thanks to more than a million members and the dedicated efforts of our diverse staff and over 400 scientists, we impact conservation in 76 countries and territories: 37 by direct conservation impact and 39 through partners.
